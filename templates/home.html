Violent Protests in Bangladesh Over Quota Reform

Protests demanding quota reform in Bangladesh for government jobs have turned violent. More than 5 students have died, and over 500 have been injured by the Police and Bangladesh Chhatra League (BCL).

This project aims to document all attacks carried out by the police and BCL on students.

If you’re a protester and something is happening in your area, please take a photo or video and report it to us using the Report Option in the navbar.
